About Me

I design intuitive, human-centered products by combining a decade of visual storytelling experience with research-driven UX strategy.

Before product design, I spent 9 years designing for Governant Agancies and Public Universities at Nickelodeon, Warner Bros, Disney, and Cartoon Network. That background sharpened my ability to communicate complex ideas visually, collaborate cross-functionally, and build systems that feel purposeful and delightful.

Now I bring that same strategic eye to crafting digital experiences that are not just functional, but meaningful. I specialize in research synthesis, UX flows, and information architecture, grounded in a deep understanding of human behavior and story.

I use design to thoughtfully consider every angle — business, ethics, innovation, and human connection — to create products that make life better.
